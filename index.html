About

Hello! My name is Dr. Kalyanmoy Chatterjee and I have a strong background in physics. I obtained my M.Sc. in Physics in 2009 from Visva-Bharati, with a specialization in Astrophysics and Cosmology. I went on to pursue a PhD in Experimental High Energy Physics, which I completed in 2016 at SINP. My doctoral research focused on the search for the Standard Model Higgs boson, a fundamental particle cornerstone of the Standard Model of particle physics. During my PhD, I also acquired a range of computing and machine learning skills.

After completing my PhD, I spent two years (2016-2018) as a postdoctoral researcher in Experimental High Energy Physics, where I worked on the development of detector hardware. This challenging and exciting experience allowed me to apply my research skills to a new area.

Currently, I am working as a physics teacher at VC College. I find teaching to be an incredibly rewarding profession, as it allows me to share my knowledge and enthusiasm for physics with the next generation of scientists.

In addition to teaching, I have a strong interest in fintech and am working on building an AI-based investment company. I believe that combining my expertise in physics with cutting-edge AI and machine learning techniques can lead to innovative solutions in the financial industry.

Overall, I feel incredibly fortunate to have had the opportunities to pursue my passions in physics and technology. I am excited to see what the future holds and look forward to contributing to advancements in both fields.
